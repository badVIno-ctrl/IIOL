Open Aura
01

Do I need a finished brand guide?

No. Aura can start with a screenshot, a rough product note, a color direction, or a DESIGN.md file. Better context gives better drafts, but you do not need a complete system before you begin.
02

Can Aura use screenshots or existing sites as references?

Yes. Upload imagery or paste URLs, and the vision model will extract structural layouts, padding rhythms, and typographic hierarchy to inform the initial generation prompt.
03

Can I export the page?

Absolutely. Aura generates raw HTML, React components, and standard Tailwind CSS. You own the code. Copy it directly or connect your repository.
04

What if the first generation misses?

Iterate locally. Use natural language to adjust sections, tweak variables in the visual editor, or replace the context document and regenerate specific components without losing the whole layout.
